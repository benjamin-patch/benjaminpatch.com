        Filmmaker for Hire

        Hi There!


        I am a filmmaker who enjoys taking a concept and transforming it into a story that resonates with target audiences. I invest a great deal of time into understanding the businesses and organizations who hire me, and then propose video content strategies to best help achieve their goals.

        When not working with clients, I write, host, and direct filmmaking tutorials for Celestial Cinema. Previously, I ran a multimedia design and production company called Cascading Media and served as an intelligence analyst for The United States Army. I graduated with honors from Binghamton University with a Bachelor of Arts in Cinema.

        I currently live outside Philadelphia with my beautiful wife, Leslie. If you are interested in working on a film or video project together, please contact me.
