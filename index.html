Film Director and Screenwriter

Benjamin Patch is a filmmaker who currently writes, hosts, and produces film and video editing tutorials for The Post Workshop. Previously he ran a multimedia design and production company called Cascading Media and served as an intelligence analyst for The United States Army. Benjamin Patch graduated with honors from Binghamton University with a Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Cinema.

Astray

Awarded "Best Student Short" by the
2019 International New York Film Festival

Written and Directed by Benjamin Patch

"Astray" is an award-winning 25-minute film that centers around a college student who has an extraordinary ability to “see” the future potential of his fellow students. It is currently being adapted into a feature-length screenplay by Benjamin Patch.
