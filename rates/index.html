        2024 Rates


        My current rates are listed below. Please note, I generally do not accept projects with budgets less than $1,000. All rates are listed in US dollars.

        • Hourly Rate: $75
        • Half day (up to 4-hours): $250 (save 17% from hourly rate)
        • Full day (up to 8-hours): $500 (save 17% from hourly rate)
        Shooting and editing services are billed at the same rate. A one-third (1/3) non-refundable deposit is required to reserve services for a specific date and/or location.

        Travel greater than one hour from Irvine, CA will incur additional charges.

        Dormant Project Fee


        Projects that are awaiting client feedback and/or approval for more than seven (7) calendar days will be charged $50 per day (starting on day 8) until editing can resume. This is because high-quality video and audio consume an enormous amount of drive space that cannot be used for other work. I appreciate your understanding.
